Steak Stir Fry

Description

This is my favorite dish to cook, because it is easy and relatively healthy. I could almost eat this dish every day.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Rice Noodles
  • Broccoli
  • White Mushrooms
  • A Large Wok
  • High Smoke Point Oil

Steps

  1. Get a pot and fill it with about 3 cups of water.
  2. Place pot on burner and bring to a boil.
  3. Once boiling, place noodle in pot and cook until soft.
  4. Pour noodles into a collander to drain, and run cold water over them to stop the cooking process.
  5. Slice beef to bite size strips or chunks.
  6. Cut mushrooms and broccoli to bite size chunks as well.
  7. Place wok on burner and get warm.
  8. Put enough oil in wok to cover bottom of pan, then swirl it to cover cooking surface.
  9. Place veggies in the wok all together and keep stirring on high heat.
  10. Once veggies have been cooking for a minute or so, add the strips of beef to the wok.
  11. After the beef begins browning, add some salt and pepper to taste.
  12. Add noodles to wok after draining all water.
  13. Keep stirring until noodles get some good color.
  14. Stir a little longer and then add soy sauce.
  15. Place food and enjoy.
