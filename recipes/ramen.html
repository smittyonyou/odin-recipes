Instant Ramen

Description

This is a super easy dish to cook, and tasty as well.

Ingredients

  • Instant Ramen Pack
  • Pot
  • Collander
  • Water

Steps

  1. Get a pot and fill it with about 3 cups of water.
  2. Place pot on burner and bring to a boil.
  3. Once boiling, place noodle in pot and cook until soft.
  4. Drain some of water into Collander.
  5. Add ramen flavor pack and serve.
