Steak on Cast Iron

Description

This is my favorite dish to eat, I love steak.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Cast Iron Pan
  • Grapeseed Oil, or any other high heat oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Lay steak out and season with salt and pepper generously.
  2. Place cast iron on high heat.
  3. Coat bottom of pan with oil.
  4. Once oil is very hot, place steak in pan.
  5. Cook for 2.5 minutes on each side.
  6. Let rest for 10 minutes, then serve
  7. Enjoy!
